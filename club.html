VIT Clubs

IEEE VIT

IEEE VIT empowers students by organizing international-level tech workshops, industrial visits, innovation challenges, and paper publishing opportunities across various domains of engineering and computing.

GetIT VIT

The GetIT club at VIT encourages competitive coding through contests, hands-on sessions, and mentorship, helping students improve algorithmic thinking and problem-solving skills for national and global competitions.

TRF Club

TRF Club provides a platform for students to explore mechanical design, embedded systems, and AI-powered automation through the construction of real-world robotic systems for competitions and innovation fairs.

Zephyr Club

The Zephyr Club fosters creativity and expression through rigorous training and vibrant performances, participating in campus fests, cultural shows, and inter-university dance competitions throughout the year.

Aaroh Club

VIT's Aaroh Club is a Music community of singers, instrumentalists, and producers that organizes open mics, concerts, and studio recordings, promoting talent in both classical and contemporary music genres.

Viculp Club

The Viculp Club of VIT brings together budding actors, directors, and scriptwriters to create compelling stage productions, street plays, and short films focused on social issues and creative storytelling.

NSS

NSS VIT encourages students to engage in social service by organizing blood donation camps, awareness rallies, tree plantations, and rural education programs to give back to the community.
